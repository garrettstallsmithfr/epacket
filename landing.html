What to do in case of an accident?

• Notify police and obtain accident report

• Seek medical help for injured parties

• Do not admit fault

• Call us at 800.338.0619 to file a claim

• Obtain information from any witness and/or injured parties

• Obtain driver and insurance information from other parties involved

• Fleet Response can arrange a tow for your vehicle, repair any damage, provide rental replacement, and glass service
